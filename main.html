Checkers Game Instructions

1. Checkers is played on an 8x8 board with 12 pieces on each side.
2. The pieces move diagonally and can only move forward until they are "kinged".
3. A piece is kinged when it reaches the farthest row from the player.
4. Kings can move forward and backward diagonally.
5. A piece can capture an opponent's piece by jumping over it diagonally.
6. The game is won by capturing all of the opponent's pieces or by blocking them so they cannot move.
